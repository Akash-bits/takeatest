Who am I?

I am Akash Reddy, a student of BITS Pilani, Goa campus, pursuing Computer Science.So what makes me bring you this is the story of my preparation for these competitive exams.
Throughout those two enriching years I worked super hard, was super serious and I had the immense trust of my teachers that I would succeed in JEE. I was more than well prepared for my tests but .... I underperformed in JEE Main.I introspected and realised that though my problem solving skills were extraordinary and my theory was good , I hardly gave any 'serious' mock tests. And what followed was BITSAT, I swear didnt even touch my books for those 20 days before BITSAT rather I gave 2 mock tests each day and the result I was supercool on the D-day(as opposed to the Mains day where I was super anxious) and scored a magnificient 371.

So to prevent my juniors and serious aspirants make the same mistake, I got this site up.
